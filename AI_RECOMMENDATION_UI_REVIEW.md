I treated the block surface as two UIs: the main block panel in [BlockRecommendationsPanel.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/BlockRecommendationsPanel.js#L444) and the styles subpanel in [StylesRecommendations.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/StylesRecommendations.js#L79).

**Findings**
1. The executable/advisory split is only fully standardized on the preview-first surfaces. Block uses `Apply now` vs `Manual ideas`, block styles is executable-only, template/template-part/style-book/global-styles use `Review first` vs `Manual ideas`, and pattern has no lane split at all. Within the files you named, [Prompt.php](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/inc/LLM/Prompt.php#L18) is block-specific and hard-codes advisory-only structural/pattern ideas at [Prompt.php](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/inc/LLM/Prompt.php#L74) and strips their executable updates at [Prompt.php](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/inc/LLM/Prompt.php#L474). Block then renders `Apply now`/`Manual ideas` lanes at [BlockRecommendationsPanel.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/BlockRecommendationsPanel.js#L533), while template/template-part derive `Review first` from `canApply` at [TemplateRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/templates/TemplateRecommender.js#L433) and [TemplatePartRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/template-parts/TemplatePartRecommender.js#L577), and style-book/global-styles derive it from `tone === 'executable'` at [StyleBookRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/style-book/StyleBookRecommender.js#L223) and [GlobalStylesRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/global-styles/GlobalStylesRecommender.js#L209).

2. Advisory lane component reuse is inconsistent. Block, style-book, and global-styles use `RecommendationLane` for both lanes, but template and template-part switch their advisory side to the collapsible `AIAdvisorySection`, which has different toggle/show-more behavior and a hidden-by-default advisory badge. See [BlockRecommendationsPanel.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/BlockRecommendationsPanel.js#L554), [StyleBookRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/style-book/StyleBookRecommender.js#L435), [GlobalStylesRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/global-styles/GlobalStylesRecommender.js#L418), [TemplateRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/templates/TemplateRecommender.js#L725), [TemplatePartRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/template-parts/TemplatePartRecommender.js#L879), and [AIAdvisorySection.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/components/AIAdvisorySection.js#L8).

3. The review-before-apply contract is explicit in store, but the UI still drifts. The shared contract makes block the only inline-apply surface and marks template/template-part/style-book/global-styles as preview-required at [store/index.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/store/index.js#L162). Block applies directly from chips and rejects advisory items in the action layer at [BlockRecommendationsPanel.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/BlockRecommendationsPanel.js#L545) and [store/index.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/store/index.js#L1900). The other executable surfaces all require `Review` first, then `AIReviewSection`, then confirm, but template/template-part keep `Confirm Apply` while style-book/global-styles rename it to `Apply Style Change`. See [TemplateRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/templates/TemplateRecommender.js#L759), [TemplatePartRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/template-parts/TemplatePartRecommender.js#L913), [StyleBookRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/style-book/StyleBookRecommender.js#L451), and [GlobalStylesRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/global-styles/GlobalStylesRecommender.js#L434).

4. Undo and history are not actually consistent. The shared activity component supports undo status, pending-sync states, and overflow expansion at [AIActivitySection.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/components/AIActivitySection.js#L49) and [AIActivitySection.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/components/AIActivitySection.js#L181). Block, style-book, and global-styles pass fuller histories into it, but template and template-part pre-truncate to the latest 3 items before the component sees them, so their `Show more` path can never expose older actions. See [BlockRecommendationsPanel.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/BlockRecommendationsPanel.js#L381), [StyleBookRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/style-book/StyleBookRecommender.js#L480), [GlobalStylesRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/global-styles/GlobalStylesRecommender.js#L463), [TemplateRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/templates/TemplateRecommender.js#L278), and [TemplatePartRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/template-parts/TemplatePartRecommender.js#L435). Block is also the only surface that injects request diagnostics into history for empty results at [BlockRecommendationsPanel.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/BlockRecommendationsPanel.js#L326).

5. Freshness detection is broadly shared, but stale-state UX is not. Block, template, template-part, style-book, and global-styles all compare stored result refs/signatures to live context and show `SurfaceScopeBar` freshness, but only block also swaps its executable lane tone to `Stale` and shows a dedicated stale hero. StylesRecommendations freshness-protects apply by passing the live block signature, but surfaces no stale banner/scope bar, and pattern has no freshness affordance at all. See [BlockRecommendationsPanel.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/BlockRecommendationsPanel.js#L252), [BlockRecommendationsPanel.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/BlockRecommendationsPanel.js#L510), [TemplateRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/templates/TemplateRecommender.js#L373), [TemplatePartRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/template-parts/TemplatePartRecommender.js#L520), [StyleBookRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/style-book/StyleBookRecommender.js#L692), [GlobalStylesRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/global-styles/GlobalStylesRecommender.js#L646), [StylesRecommendations.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/StylesRecommendations.js#L34), and [PatternRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/patterns/PatternRecommender.js#L645).

6. The biggest copy/UX gaps are on pattern, block styles, and the style surfaces after first use. Pattern has no `SurfaceComposer`, starter prompts, scope bar, review section, or history because it rides the inserter search and a portal notice instead of the shared shell at [PatternRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/patterns/PatternRecommender.js#L525). StylesRecommendations has no composer, scope bar, review section, history, or capability notice of its own at [StylesRecommendations.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/StylesRecommendations.js#L82). Style-book and global-styles hide helper text and lane guidance once `showSecondaryGuidance` is false, while block/template/template-part keep that guidance visible continuously. See [StyleBookRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/style-book/StyleBookRecommender.js#L235), [StyleBookRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/style-book/StyleBookRecommender.js#L425), [GlobalStylesRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/global-styles/GlobalStylesRecommender.js#L220), [GlobalStylesRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/global-styles/GlobalStylesRecommender.js#L355), [BlockRecommendationsPanel.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/inspector/BlockRecommendationsPanel.js#L474), [TemplateRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/templates/TemplateRecommender.js#L629), and [TemplatePartRecommender.js](/home/hperkins-wp/htdocs/wp.hperkins.com/wp-content/plugins/flavor-agent/src/template-parts/TemplatePartRecommender.js#L783).
